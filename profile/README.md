## 🔒 What is LuaGuard?

LuaGuard is a modern authentication and script protection platform built for Cfx.re developers.

Created by the team behind stigstream.ru, LuaGuard focuses on helping developers secure their work against leaks, unauthorized usage, and redistribution.

From simple whitelist keys to full customer management and banning systems, LuaGuard gives you complete control over your scripts.

## 📦 Does LuaGuard Store My Scripts?

No.

LuaGuard does **not** store your source code on the panel. Your original scripts remain entirely in your control.

We only host the protected output files that are generated for distribution to your customers.
